The official SIDEUP for WooCommerce plugin allows you to automate your e-commerce order process.

== Description ==

SIDEUP's official extension for WooCommerce on Wordpress in Egypt.

== Features ==

1. Easy **setup** and **use**.
1. Well designed dashboard for your woocommerce orders.
1. Full dashboard on **our system** for all details about your account.
1. You will place your order and print its bill and track it from the same place.
1. Account manager that you contact through any issues or if you need more information or requesting services.
1. This app is to make your eCommerce experience easier and faster. If you have any suggestions don't hesitate to contact us.

== Installation & Configuration ==

1. Upload the downloaded plugin files to your `/wp-content/plugins/sideup-woocommerce` directory, **OR** install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
1. Go to WooCommerce-->Settings->Shipping and select the upper SIDEUP unit to configure the plugin.
1. Add your business APIKEY that you will find it in your dashboard in our system in **integrations** tab or call your **Account manager**.
